ABOUT



Government Kannada Higher Primary School No.26,Belagavi,was founded in 1980.

Our school provides education for students from 1st std to 8th std.It has both Kannada as well as English medium, which helps the students to understand the concepts easily in their mother tongue as well as develops their listening,reading and writing skills in English.

Everyday, the school begins with daily assembly, consisting of prayer after which the regular classes begin.During games period,the students can play a variety of indoor as well as outdoor sports.

The Number of faculties in the Teaching department are 10.The current Head Mistress is Mrs. R.N Belgaonkar. The total number of students for the current academic year 2022-23 sums up to 316.

ABOUT NMMS(National Means Cum-Merit Scholarship) EXAM:
NMMS is a National level competitive scholarship examination scheme for 8th standard students who are studying in Government and Government-aided schools.Grants scholarship of INR 1000 per month for 4 years from class IX to XII. Students will develop the competitive skills to face the competitive and government exams with confidence.

ADMISSIONS


For the admission of students between 1st std to 8th std, the following documents are required:

  1. Original Birth Certificate of the child
  2. Aadhar Card of the child
  3. Aadhar Card of both the parents
  4. Caste certificate of father
  5. Duly filled Admission application form
  6. Other documents,as required
